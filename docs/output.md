**Overall takeaway**  
2026 marks the transition from “search‑first” to **“AI‑first”** search: generative AI now dominates the top of the results page, zero‑click answers are the norm, and visibility hinges on being an **authoritative, well‑structured source** that AI systems can confidently cite. Traditional SEO remains important for crawlability, but success now requires a blend of **E‑E‑A‑T, structured markup, and GEO tactics**, plus awareness of emerging paid‑in‑answer opportunities.
